For years, @FrostFoe and @thnbangladesh have been working extensively in the field of cybersecurity. Our mission is to raise awareness about cybersecurity and technology to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge needed to stay safe in the digital world. Despite facing numerous challenges along the way, we have remained committed to our cause, and the support from our community has been a constant source of motivation.

Following the closure of our previous Telegram channel, we have relaunched our platform with renewed strength and dedication. We continue to move forward with a clear objective: to educate and empower individuals with essential cybersecurity and technology knowledge. We are grateful for the unwavering support of our community and invite everyone to join us on this journey of learning, growth, and a safer digital future.
